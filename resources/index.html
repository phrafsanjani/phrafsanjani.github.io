Books

I have always been an avid reader and a book lover. When I was younger, I enjoyed fiction more than anything else. However, I have now become too picky, and I only opt for the very good stories when it comes to fiction! While I am trying to get back to reading fiction, most of my reading consists of non-fiction and scientific texts and papers.
